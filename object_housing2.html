OBJECTIVES OF HOUSING ASSOCIATION 2

This player is one of two Housing Associations. Housing associations must maintain the sanitary water systems in the houses they own. These systems are responsible for providing water to the occupants of the houses. There are two different types of water system that housing associations can buy from manufacturers: normal or water saving. Since housing associations must pay for the water their houses' tenants consume, the choice of water system affects the associations' profits. Water systems must be bought to replace old systems that after a specific number of years, breakdown.

Goal

  • Maximize profits and be the most successful Housing Association

Tasks

  • call for quotations to install new water systems.
    Hint: bargaining with manufacturers can reduce prices and negotiating with politicians can help you subsidize purchases and influence manufacturers to lower their prices.
  • choose to buy normal or water saving systems
    Hint: water saving systems could reduce water bills and maximize profits.

Income sources

  • housing rents

Expenses

  • maintenance of house water systems, paying of water bills and purchasing of new water systems
