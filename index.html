THE ZURICH INTERNET WATER GAME AS A LEARNING TOOL

The Zürich Internet Water Game has been developed as part of an EU Research Programme called the Firma Project.

It was designed in order that researchers and stakeholders can use gameplaying to better understand the problems of water management and how they can resolved.

 

THE ZURICH INTERNET WATER GAME AS A GAME

The game was designed specifically for stakeholders to play.

Each game player adopts a role involved in water management, these include water utility companies, manufacturers, housing associations and politicians. The game presents each player with many of the real-life demands of water management thereby highlighting the issues affecting decision making.

By analysing how each player responds to these demands and issues we can attempt to advance the management of water.
